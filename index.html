Who He Is

A Life of
Leadership & Stewardship

Mohanlal Ghewarchand Bhandari is a qualified Chartered Accountant with decades of experience spanning finance, consulting, banking, governance, and business leadership. Through a distinguished professional journey, he has contributed significantly to corporate stewardship, advisory, and institutional growth across sectors.

Known for principled leadership and strategic insight, he has served on the boards of respected institutions including RBL Bank and Everest Industries Ltd., while contributing to long-term business development through MGB Group entities.

His career reflects a quiet but enduring commitment to building institutions of lasting value — in commerce, in governance, and in community.
